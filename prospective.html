About:

Welcome to Placement and Training cell of MNIT Jaipur. The T&P cell is mainly responsible for Facilitating Industrial Training, mandatory for UG (B.Tech.) curriculum. Inviting Companies/Institutions/Organizations of repute for placements of both UG and PG students. The T&P cell collaborates with leading organizations for setting up internship and training program of students.The Office interacts with many companies/industries in the country, of which nearly 200 companies visit the campus for holding campus interviews. These organizations which approach the institute come under the purview of: Core Engineering Industries, IT & IT enabled services, Manufacturing, Consultancy Firms, Investment & Finance Companies, Management Organisations, R & D laboratories. Job offers, dates of interviews, selection of candidates etc. are announced through the Training & Placement Office.




The placement season runs through the course of the year commencing the first week of August through to March. The Campus Placements of MNIT Jaipurfor the session 2018-19 will start from July 25, 2018.
